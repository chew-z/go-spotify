About

I listen to music on Spotify for hours every day and I have made this companion app for myself.

Spotify is great but I find it's interface overloaded and changing too often. I wanted easy access to my recently listened tracks, top tracks, my playlists and albums.

I also like to keep myself in a musical mood so I have made a simple tool for recommending songs based on my recently listened five tracks and audio attributes of last 24 tracks. Now you don't have to wait for Discovery Weekly on Monday.

You can also see basic audio attributes (tempo, loudness, energy, accousticness, instrumentalness) of tracks in a playlist, album or history.

This is work in progress, it may occassionaly fail or run into errors. Please be gentle. I am planning to add good search and other features
